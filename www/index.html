Sudoku Solver

Instructions:

Enter known values into their respective boxes.

Click solve to instruct the computer to calculate the solution.

Once the puzzle is solved, double click on a square to reveal is value.

Alternatively, Upload pictures of puzzles to solve by clicking on the upload box. Make sure the extracted values are correct before solving.

Buttons:

  • Solve: Solves the puzzle but does not show the answers
  • Show: Shows the answer to the puzzle
  • Clear: Clears the values from the puzzle
  • Reset: Reload the last unsoved inputed puzzle
  • Check: Reveal incorrect values
